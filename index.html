Sarc7

Evaluating Sarcasm Detection & Generation with Seven Types and Emotion-Informed Techniques

💡 About the Paper

Sarc7 is a benchmark dataset that evaluates sarcasm across seven fine-grained types. It includes emotional cues and supports classification and generation. Our work improves sarcasm detection and interpretability in NLP.

📄 Paper Overview

🎯 Tasks

  • Binary sarcasm detection
  • Subtype classification
  • Generation from literal to sarcastic text

📦 Dataset

Includes 10,000+ annotated posts with:

  • 7 sarcasm types
  • Emotion labels
  • Text spans & silver/gold labels

🤖 Models

Fine-tuned BERT and RoBERTa with multi-task learning. Emotion embeddings integrated into transformer heads.

📊 Results

Emotion-aware models outperform all baselines by 5–10%. Best subtype: rhetorical. Generation quality improves with emotion supervision.
