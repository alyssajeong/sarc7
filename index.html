Sarc7

Evaluating Sarcasm Detection & Generation with Seven Types and Emotion-Informed Techniques

📝 About

Sarc7 is a benchmark dataset for detecting and generating sarcasm. It includes seven nuanced sarcasm types and emotion-informed labels, enabling research in multi-task learning and interpretability. Designed with both linguistic theory and psychological cues, Sarc7 is a unique contribution to understanding sarcasm in NLP.

🎯 Tasks

  • Sarcasm Detection — Classify sarcastic vs. non-sarcastic content
  • Subtype Classification — Predict specific sarcasm categories
  • Generation — Rewrite literal statements with sarcastic intent

📦 Dataset

The dataset includes over 10,000 social media posts annotated with:

  • 7 sarcasm types (e.g. hyperbole, rhetorical question)
  • Emotion labels: anger, amusement, annoyance, etc.
  • Text spans and generation references

🤖 Models

We evaluate BERT, RoBERTa, and multi-task transformers with subtype classification and generation heads. Emotion-informed embeddings improve sarcasm subtype predictions.
