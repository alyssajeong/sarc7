Sarc7

Evaluating Sarcasm Detection and Generation with Seven Types and Emotion-Informed Techniques

About

Sarc7 is a benchmark for sarcasm detection and generation. It features seven distinct sarcasm types and emotion-informed annotation, enabling both classification and generation tasks. Our goal is to improve sarcasm understanding by integrating psychological and linguistic theory.

Tasks

  • Detection: Classify posts as sarcastic or not, and by subtype
  • Generation: Rewrite or produce sarcastic utterances from literal statements
  • Multi-task: Jointly learn emotion prediction, subtype detection, and sarcasm intent

Dataset

The dataset includes labeled sarcastic and non-sarcastic social media posts, annotated with:

  • One of 7 sarcasm types (e.g., hyperbole, rhetorical question)
  • Emotional cues (e.g., frustration, mockery)
  • Gold and silver standard labels

Models

We evaluate several transformer-based architectures (BERT, RoBERTa) with multi-task heads for subtype classification and sarcasm generation. Emotion-informed embeddings are integrated to improve subtle sarcasm type detection.
