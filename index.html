About the Paper

Sarc7: Evaluating Sarcasm Detection and Generation with Seven Types and Emotion-Informed Techniques presents a new benchmark for sarcasm understanding. The dataset includes over 10,000 posts annotated with 7 fine-grained sarcasm types (e.g., rhetorical question, understatement). The paper introduces emotion-informed modeling techniques, explores multi-task learning, and provides detailed generation and classification evaluations. This resource encourages more nuanced understanding of sarcasm in NLP models.
